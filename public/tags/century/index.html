Going over the Waccamaw river bridge in Georgetown the rain felt like needles. The wind for most of the ride was a favorable cross tailwind. On the easterly section of Saints Delight Rd and coming into Georgetown it was a stiff headwind. Traffic on Hwy 17 from Georgetown to Myrtle Beach was heavy with plenty of water ponding.
