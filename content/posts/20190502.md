The commute was good but afterwards was better. I picked up Old Yeller from Mike's Bikes. I got a new Shimano Tiagra derailleur and a cable, for free. It was free because the derailleur hanger snapped Saturday and destroyed the derailleur. Mike's Bikes has 30 day warranty on repair work. üëç This is on top of the new cassette and chain from maintenance the week before. I paid for that work. Super stoked! I hope the rain stays off shore this weekend. 

2019 is going OK. I have 3,100 miles on the log. I'm a little behind pace for my goal of 10,000 this year. I need to pick it up a bit.
