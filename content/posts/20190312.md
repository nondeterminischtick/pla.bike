Beautiful weather today made for an enjoyable commute. Except for bubba yelling at me to get off the f'ing road at Black Tom Rd & Hwy 17-A intersection it was a chill ride home.

My Anker 10,000mAh battery only lasted about 5 months. I've been attempting to charge it for 3 days without luck. The way I mount it on my bike it catches sweat so the connectors are corroded. Probably will buy another and flip it upside down this time. Using the battery for my GoPro Hero 6 camera. This big battery is so much nicer than having to change GoPro batteries every hour.
