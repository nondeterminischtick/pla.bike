I thought I was going to take the day off the bike but I got the itch and had to scratch it.

Ride started at 4 PM which put me riding home in the dark. Around the lake it was foggy and misty. I startled several white tailed deer below the dike. They scurried off into the woods. The dike was pretty muddy and slow going in spots. The wind was favorable for the most part. Average temperature was 52.9 Â°F. Good ride.
