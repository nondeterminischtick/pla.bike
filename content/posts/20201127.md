Didn't feel like riding a century mentally or physically this morning. Got on the bike anyway to take advantage of this warm weather. Headed out to Ravenel SC and thought about heading home several times but told myself just keep pedaling. As the miles ticked away, the headwind changed to a tailwind and the temperature cracked 70â„‰, there was no reason to go home. Amazing how that works.

Rode a few new-to-me quiet country roads today. Nice! Century ride 102 turned out to be a good one.

273 miles short of my 10k goal for 2020. Will I ride for the 5th day in a row? I'm guessing no but look what happened today.
