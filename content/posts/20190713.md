I love McClellanville. Such a beautiful coastal town. Fantastic homes on the tree lined Pinckney St.

My fortieth century had a brutally tough finish. My legs were cramping and I was running on empty the last two hours of this 8 hour ride. Had to stop to stretch and recover a couple of times. I never had to do that before. Here's to the next 40 less painful century rides. üç∫
